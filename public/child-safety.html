TCGtrade - Child Safety Standards

1. Zero-Tolerance Policy

TCGTrade has a strict zero-tolerance policy for any form of Child Sexual Abuse Material (CSAM) or exploitation of minors. We are committed to maintaining a safe environment for all users.

2. Reporting Harmful Content

Users can report suspected CSAM or child safety concerns directly within the app through the in-app "Report" feature, or by contacting us via email at havihavi@live.com.

3. Response Process

All reports are reviewed by our moderation team within 24 hours. If CSAM is confirmed, the content is removed immediately, the offending accounts are banned, and relevant authorities are notified.

4. Cooperation with Authorities

We comply with all applicable child protection laws and report confirmed CSAM cases to national and regional law enforcement agencies, including cybercrime units, as required by law.

5. Prevention Measures

We use automated systems and manual review to detect and block harmful content, and we continuously update our safety measures to improve detection and prevention.
